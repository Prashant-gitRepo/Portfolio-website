Experimental Investigation of Nano coolant while Turning operation on CNC Machine by using MQL and Taguchi Method.

Role - Group Head

Review Paper - Link

Keywords

Taguchi Methode MQL (Minimum Quantity Lubrication) Turning Operation Nano coolant

What We Did

  • Here in this research work, we have proposed Nano cutting fluid along with Minimum Quantity Lubrication (MQL).
  • We have synthesized Al2O3 Nanoparticle and mix with water as well as Paraffin oil as a base fluid.
  • Experimentation has been done using the “Taguchi” method.
  • L9 array has been adopted for the turning operation.
  • An Experiment has also been done with dry machining and conventional flood cooling system.
  • Turning operation was done on EN-8 Medium carbon steel.
  • The effect on different coolant (flood cutting, dry cutting, and MQL) on surface roughness of turning operation on EN8 steel are investigated, and the result of all has compared.
  • After turning operation and surface finish testing we found to achieve great surface finish of a product we need best cooling technology and best speed for work piece and tool.
