Study of Different types of Gears (Mini Project).

Role - Team Member

Gear Types Covered:

Spur Gear Helical Gear Rack and Pinion Bevel Gear Worm Gear

What We Did

  • Conducted theoretical research on gear profiles and gear ratios.
  • Studied torque transmission, efficiency, and direction of rotation.
  • Compared gear types based on use-cases, advantages, and limitations.
  • Analyzed real-life gear applications in automobiles, machines, and robotics.
  • Studied material properties (e.g., cast iron, alloy steel, bronze) for different gear applications.
  • Documented findings in a well-structured report with diagrams and charts.
  • Presented the project in front of peers/faculty with technical explanations and Q&A.
